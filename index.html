Welcome To TransKribe

A Spin-off of oTranscribe.

TransKribe is an online website used for transcribing audio files.

Press ESC for pause and play.
Press F1 for rewinding.
Press F2 for fast forwarding.
Press F3 for inserting interactive timestamps.

Afraid of losing the text?
Saves the text automatically
to the browser's storage
every second.

Formating tools
for the text typed.

Interactive timestamps
to navigate through
the audio.

Private -
The transcript never
leaves your computer.

Pause, rewind and fast-forward
without taking your
hands off the keyboard.
